We Are Specialists In

Complex Rehab

Our highly trained team of professionals works closely with our partners, therapists, and clients to design and deliver custom solutions. We are committed to providing ongoing support and service throughout the lifespan of our high-quality equipment. Our range includes the top-selling Netti 4U Tilt in space reclining wheelchair, and the TA IQ Powerchair which are suitable for indoor and outdoor use. We also offer the Dietz Slimline powerchair, ideal for compact indoor apartments, and the amazing Meyra Optimus 2 powerchair for rugged rural areas or distance travel.

Hospital

We understand that hospitals have unique needs when it comes to healthcare and rehabilitation equipment. Whether you opt for rental or purchase, we offer a comprehensive selection of mobility, sleep, and falls prevention aids, coupled with prompt delivery and dedicated support for your utmost convenience. Our range includes walkers, air pressure mattresses, wheelchairs and bathroom and daily living aids. Novis has been providing Aged Care equipment for the best clinical outcomes since 2002. We work with architects, builders, aged care facilities, and project managers to find the best solutions. Our wide range of products include the Procair alternating air mattresses, the Molift patient lifters and ceiling hoists, the Better Living nonslip floor mats and dementia support aids.

Pressure Injury Prevention
Pressure injuries typically appear in the form of a sore, or ulcer, on areas of the skin that are regularly exposed to pressure. We are determined to provide 24/7 vigilant care to patients and physicians, from specialized mattresses to air cell cushions. We help you develop a better understanding of pressure injury prevention.
